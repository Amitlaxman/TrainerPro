# **App Name**: TrainerPro

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: A comprehensive dashboard displaying key training metrics such as total trained, average scores, compliance rates, pending certifications, departmental completion progress, and upcoming training events, as seen in the reference image.
- Employee Management: Enable trainers to add, view, and manage trainee (guard worker) profiles, including their training assignments and progress.
- Course Management & Assignment: Provide functionalities to create, edit, organize, and assign training courses to individual trainees or groups, along with options to upload training materials.
- Training History & Certification Tracking: Track the full training history for each employee, monitor their certification status, and view past completed courses and examinations.
- Performance Analytics & Reporting: Generate reports and data visualizations on overall training performance, compliance rates, and individual trainee achievements, mirroring the 'Analytics' section.
- AI Training Content Tool: A generative AI tool that assists trainers by automatically summarizing long training documents or generating targeted quiz questions based on uploaded training materials.
- Notification and Alert System: Provide automated notifications and alerts for overdue training, expiring certifications, new course publications, and compliance issues, mimicking the 'Compliance Alert' and 'Recent Activity' sections.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A confident and vibrant blue (#3383ED) to signify interactive elements, call-to-actions, and highlights, maintaining a professional yet engaging feel.
- Background color: A very dark, slightly bluish-gray (#191D24) for a sophisticated and modern dark mode aesthetic that prioritizes content visibility and reduces eye strain.
- Accent color: A soft, inviting purple-blue (#8472EB) to be used sparingly for special notifications, decorative elements, or secondary interactive components, offering a pleasing contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, clean, and highly legible characteristics suitable for data-heavy dashboards and professional content.
- Use a consistent set of modern, minimalist line icons that clearly communicate their purpose without distraction, maintaining visual harmony with the clean UI.
- Implement a structured dashboard layout with a fixed left sidebar navigation and clear, distinct content cards and sections, allowing for easy scanning of information and intuitive navigation.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations for state changes (e.g., button clicks, navigation transitions) to enhance user feedback and provide a smooth, responsive user experience without being overly decorative.
